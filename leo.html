The Process

My strategic framework for F&B success.

01

Market Research

Scanning the area, defining customer personas, mapping direct competitors, and analyzing alternative solutions in the market.

02

Assessment

Establishing competitive criteria for Location, Concept, Product, and Tradezone. Validating the "Why" behind the business.

03

Growth Strategy

Defining strategic goals and financial estimations to shape Branding & Marketing objectives (Revenue, ROI, Traffic).

04

Execution (IMC)

Setting up the full communication framework: Positioning, Big Idea, Brand Archetype, Key Message, and Integrated Marketing Channels.
